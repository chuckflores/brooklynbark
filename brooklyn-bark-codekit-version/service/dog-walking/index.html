Walk Highlights

Individual Care

Each dog has their own primary care giver who is with them 99% of the time. For those odd occasions and emergencies, your pup will have his/her familar back-up walker.

GPS Tracking

Know exactly when and where your pup walked! Everything is tracked by our smartphones. Since our phones are always with us when we walk, you are seeing where your pup has been with your walker and for how long. 

Basic Training

Whether your dog is getting socialization with other dogs or basic puppy training our skilled associates can adapt to your needs. 

In Home Consultations

We know leaving your pet in the care of a stranger can be nerve-wracking, so we offer free in-home consultations before you become a client. You will be asigned a specific walker, and they'll send you notes after every visit to keep you updated on every poop, pee, and chicken bone.
